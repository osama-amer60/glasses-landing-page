How to use our product?

  • Press “open button” at the smart glasses
  • Link the smart glasses with 3rd eye Application
  • Sign up on 3rd eye App.
  • Import photos of people around the patient
  • Now, you can use it.

How to link the application with smart glasses?

  • Turn your phone’s Bluetooth on.
  • Press on the “Bluetooth button” at the smart glasses.
  • Pair the mobile with smart glasses.
  • If you’re on an Android phone, turn on ‘Location Services’.

How do you handle glass safely?

  • Hold the glass firmly in your hands.
  • Do NOT carry it over your head or under your arms.
  • Do not let any surface bump or hit the glass' edges or corners.
  • When setting glass on the floor, or other any other surface, gently place it down on the long edge

What do you need to know about the Application?

  • We can classify the users of 3rd eye application into two categories:
  • For the PATIENT to keep in touch with the guide or access to his mobile
  • For the GUIDE to ensure the safety of the patient by voice chatting with the PATIENT & know the current location of the PATIENT.
